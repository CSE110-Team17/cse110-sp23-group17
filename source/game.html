How to Play
  1. Select your avatar, take a picture if you would like.
  2. Click on the cards to attack them.
  3. Reduce the opponent fortune teller's HP bar to win.
  4. Your luck is determined by the Lucky Point system.
  5. The Lucky Point bar oscillates between negative and positive luck.
  6. Click on the cards when the Lucky Point bar is in the positive zone.
  7. Find out your fortune!
